Company «PWT»

The growing demand for high-quality drinking water in the world and the continuous development of analytical methods for detecting water pollution establish an ever-growing demand for water treatment technologies. But we have accepted this challenge, carrying out the continuous development of recognized technologies. Our many years of experience in all engineering disciplines related to the purification of drinking water is our decisive advantage both in the development of the technological process and in the construction of water treatment plants. Maximum quality and economic efficiency, as well as optimal integration of the plant under construction in the surrounding landscape are our main tasks. PWT Company designs and markets turnkey plants for treating both surface water and groundwater, as well as salt water desalination plants. The proven superiority of classical methods, along with the initiative and commitment to develop new technologies, forms the basis of our ability to offer our customers the right ways to solve their problems, today and in the future. In industrialized countries, water consumption by industrial facilities is 6-8 times higher than the consumption needed for household needs. Thus, the multiple use of water in closed cycles makes a valuable contribution to the conservation of water as a natural resource. Working in close collaboration with customers, PWT Company develops and delivers specially designed technologies for this client to treat water for technical needs. The quality of our plants, their operational reliability and the cost-effectiveness of the water they produce are our paramount tasks. In the field of water treatment for technical needs, the range of services provided by PWT Company includes water softening, removal of suspended and solid particles, heavy metals, iron, manganese, organic pollutants, and desalination of salt water. For power plants, we supply prefabricated plants for the treatment of water used in steam boilers, cooling water and condensate treatment. Ask us and we will provide you with solutions for any tasks. There are very few things in the world that are equally important for life on our planet as the responsible use of water resources. Wastewater treatment, the quality of which allows their reuse, is today not only an important step towards reusable water use, but also an important measure to protect world lakes, rivers and seas. PWT Company’s technological skills encompass mechanical, biological, chemical, and physical processes. By combining proven classic technologies with innovative ones, PWT Company succeeded in wastewater treatment in such a way that they can be reused or put back into the water channel satisfying all the necessary requirements. Ecology and economy are no longer constraining factors in this process.
