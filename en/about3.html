Company «GP Papenburg»

GP Papenburg Company is one of the largest road repair and construction companies in the world. Our company specializes in the construction, reconstruction, overhaul, repair and maintenance of roads and viaducts. The experience gained over many years of the company’s activities guarantees the high quality of road construction works in compliance with all technologies and norms. Today GP Papenburg Company is a dynamically developing company and actively cooperates with construction companies and other private customers, has a positive reputation among partners. We make repairs and construction of roads of any complexity: from the zero cycle to the commissioning of facilities. Our company successfully operates and develops in the road construction market. The successful work of our company is due to the use of a professional management team and experienced teams of workers and machine operators. The company can effectively carry out road and related work of any complexity (construction, reconstruction, repair, maintenance of roads of all categories), the design of transport facilities, the exercise of the functions of general contractor at large construction sites. All road works are carried out on the basis of proven modern technologies in accordance with building codes and rules applicable in the field of road construction. The high professional level of engineering, technical and working personnel allows us to carry out large-scale investment projects from design to full turnkey implementation with commissioning. The company has its own equipped fleet of vehicles, construction vehicles and mechanisms, as well as equipped repair and technical facilities. The company’s workforce consists of qualified engineers and workers, competent managers and economists. The company is ready to cooperate in any direction of road construction and other works. The round-the-clock operating mode allows you to perform work on time. The company has organized constant incoming quality control of components for the road surface. Our fleet of modern specialized equipment also contributes to the high quality of work: asphalt pavers, rollers, mills, vibrating plates and other machines of leading world brands, such as Wirgen, Bobcat, Terex, MAN, Hamm, Voegele, etc.
